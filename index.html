About Me

Hi, and Thank You for visiting my portfolio website. I am Phillip Pense, a full stack software developer from Austin. I currently work part-time as a web developer for a small real estate and design company in Austin but am looking for full-time employment as a software developer.

As a graduate of Woz-U Full Stack Software Development program, I am proficient in HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, and Java languages, as well as MySQL and MongoDB databases. I am also semi-proficient in their frameworks/libraries, Bootstrap, JQuery, ReactJS, Ionic, Spring, and Hibernate.

Analytical by nature, I enjoy trying to figure out how things work, often dissecting the item so I may improve on it and make it my own. I enjoy learning, especially subjects involving math, science, and technology.

Being from Texas, I love anything football. My freshman year attending Texas Tech, I quarterbacked our fraternity intramural football team to a Red Division Championship with the help of some very talented fraternity brothers. Today, I mostly just enjoy watching (yelling at) my favorite football teams and participating in as many fantasy football leagues I can join.

Besides being a die-hard football fan, I enjoy everything Central Texas has to offer. Live music. Good food. A cool patio. And, golf. Nothing helps wind down a long week better than listening to live music and drinking a few suds with friends on a cool outdoor patio. That... or golf.

I try not to take myself too serious and can often be seen making fun of myself. I am an open book. If you wish to know more about me, just ask. You may reach me using the contact form below, or email me direct at phillip {dot} t {dot} pense {at} gmail {dot} com. (Don't forget my middle initial.)

~ Phillip
